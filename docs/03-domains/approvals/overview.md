# Approvals Overview

Operational approvals exist as a separate workflow from the core RFQ comparison path. They may support related approval stories, but they are not the main alpha proof surface unless explicitly included by the release.

Nexus dependencies:
- Layer 1: `packages/PolicyEngine`
- Layer 2: `orchestrators/ApprovalOperations`
- Layer 3: `adapters/Laravel/ApprovalOperations`
