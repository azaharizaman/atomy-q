# Out Of Scope

The following are not part of the current Atomy-Q alpha commitment unless explicitly re-approved:

- SSO and WebAuthn
- full MFA enrollment productization
- subscription billing and payment flows
- integrations marketplace or connector rollout
- reporting modules and scheduled exports
- negotiation workflows
- risk workflows
- recommendation and scenario tooling
- vendor portal login
- broad settings write surfaces outside the minimal users/roles flow
- comparison lock/unlock and scoring-model controls beyond the narrow alpha path

Projects and tasks are optional contextual features, not core alpha blockers.
